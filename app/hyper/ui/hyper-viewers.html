To inject instrumentation debugging capabilities into a viewer, click the Start Instrumentation button for that viewer. You will then see a list of instrumentation plugins that has been loaded on the viewer. Instrumentation plugins are small JavaScript objects that enable the workbench to drill down into a hierarchy and subscribe to live data feeds from objects in that heirarchy. An example is the sample cordova plugin, which exposes a shallow heirarchy of just two objects - compass and accelerometer.

To explore the data of an instrumentation plugin, just click on each level until you get to a data object, when you click on that you will start subscribing to its data feed.

Inject Files let you upload files directly to your app running in the viewer without reloading it. Please note that you need to code a little bit yourself to take care of the file after it is uploaded, if you're using Base64 transfer.
